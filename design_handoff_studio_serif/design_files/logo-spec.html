05

Do & don't

✓ Do

Keep clay brackets with an ink (or cream) S. Maintain the bracket spacing.

× Don't

Don't recolor the brackets, tighten them onto the letter, or set the S upright (it stays italic).

× Don't

Don't skew, stretch, add shadows, or drop the terracotta full-stop from the wordmark.
