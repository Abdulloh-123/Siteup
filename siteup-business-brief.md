# Siteup Business And Website Brief

## Business Idea

Siteup is a side-hustle digital service business that builds websites, apps, systems, and practical AI workflows for small local businesses.

The first target market is small construction companies, especially businesses with no online presence or a bad existing presence. Siteup should still be positioned broadly enough to work with all kinds of local businesses, not only construction.

## Core Positioning

Siteup builds websites and digital systems for local businesses.

Homepage positioning:

> Websites and digital systems for local businesses.

Supporting copy:

> Siteup builds professional websites, online presence setups, custom apps, and practical AI workflows for construction companies, service businesses, and growing local brands.

Siteup should lead with websites as the main service, but also show that it can build:

- custom apps
- custom business systems
- automated workflows
- custom AI agents
- agentic automated workflows

## What Siteup Does Not Do

Siteup does not offer:

- social media management
- Meta ads
- Google ads
- AI phone agents

Siteup is not an ad agency. The offer is about building the website, online presence, lead flow, apps, and automation systems that help a business look professional and operate better.

## Main Call To Action

Primary CTA:

> Request Free Consultation

The CTA should open or scroll to a form on the website.

## Consultation Form

The consultation form should collect:

- Name
- Business name
- Phone
- Email
- Business type
- What do you need?
- Message

Phone or email is required. The customer does not need to provide both.

Message is optional.

Business type should be a free text field.

The "What do you need?" field should be a dropdown with these choices:

- New Website
- Online Presence / Leads
- Website Redesign
- App
- Automated Workflow
- Other

The form should send the information as plain text to email. No Telegram lead intake is needed for Siteup's own website.

## Services

The Services section should include:

### Websites

Custom, mobile-friendly websites built around the business, services, and customer trust.

### Online Presence Setup

Google setup, indexing, Apple Maps, and business listing basics so customers can find correct information.

### Lead Capture Systems

Quote forms, contact flows, and email notifications that make customer requests easier to handle.

### Custom Apps

Dashboards, portals, and internal tools built around the way the business actually works.

### Workflow Automation

Automations that reduce repetitive tasks, organize information, and keep work moving.

### Custom AI Agents

Practical AI helpers for business tasks like summaries, drafts, organization, and repeatable processes.

## AI And Automation Positioning

AI should be explained in simple, practical business language.

Recommended wording:

> We review how your business handles repetitive tasks, then build custom automations and AI helpers to save time, reduce manual work, and keep your team organized.

Examples:

- organize incoming quote requests
- summarize customer messages
- create job notes from form submissions
- remind owners to follow up
- draft replies
- update spreadsheets or CRMs
- generate project descriptions from photos or notes
- route tasks to the right person

## Website Packages

Siteup has three fixed website packages.

These are one-time prices, not monthly subscriptions.

These fixed prices are only for website projects.

Apps, custom AI agents, custom systems, and automated workflows do not use these package prices. Pricing for those products is given after Siteup understands what needs to be built and how complex the project is.

After launch, website support is paid per request depending on the size of the problem or change.

Each package includes 30 days of post-launch bug fixes.

## Custom Product Pricing Section

The website should include a full-width section explaining that apps, AI agents, custom systems, and automated workflows are not included in the fixed website package prices.

Section message:

> Website packages have fixed prices. Apps, AI agents, internal systems, and automated workflows are different: the price depends on what the product needs to do, how many steps it has, what tools it connects with, and how complex the business process is.

The section should explain:

- custom apps are quoted after discovery
- AI agents are quoted after discovery
- automated workflows are quoted after discovery
- Siteup first reviews the idea, workflow, tools, and complexity
- the customer gets a clear custom quote before anything is built

CTA:

> Request Custom Quote

## Package 1: Launch

For businesses that need a clean website they can manage.

Includes:

- custom business website
- mobile-friendly design

- reviews/testimonials section
- basic SEO setup
- Telegram website manager
- project/gallery updates from Telegram

### Telegram Website Manager

The Telegram manager should let the business owner update website content from their phone.

It should support:

- updating phone number
- updating email
- updating service areas
- updating homepage text
- updating testimonials/reviews shown on the site
- turning an emergency/banner message on or off
- creating projects
- editing project title
- editing project photos
- editing project location
- editing project description
- deleting projects

Changes should go live immediately.

Deletion should use confirmation.

## Package 2: Growth

Price:

> $1,100

For businesses that want to be easier to find online.

Includes:

- everything in Launch
- Google Search setup
- Google indexing basics
- Google Business/Profile setup or connection
- Apple Maps setup
- stronger online presence setup
- lead notifications by email

Growth should not promise top Google rankings.

The promise should be setup and indexing, not guaranteed ranking.

Recommended wording:

> We set up the basics so Google can find, index, and understand your business.

## Package 3: Premium

Price:

> $1,500

For businesses that want a sharper brand and stronger proof.

Includes:

- everything in Growth
- logo design
- brand colors and typography
- business card design
- flyer or door hanger design
- shirt/hoodie mockup
- truck decal mockup
- brand mini-guide
- approximate past-project map with clickable pins

Premium does not include AI agent planning or custom business system planning.

Premium includes design files and mockups only. Ordering clothes, cards, decals, or printed materials is the customer's responsibility.

## Premium Project Map

The Premium package should include a special map showing past project locations.

Map behavior:

- projects appear as map pins
- when someone touches/clicks a pin, project information appears
- popup should include project photo, title, location, and short description
- locations should be approximate, not exact addresses

Reason:

Approximate locations protect customer privacy while still showing local proof.

## Visual Style

Preferred style:

- clean and professional
- black/dark background
- SaaS-style landing page
- premium dark UI
- glassy cards
- animated dashboard panels
- gradient edge accents
- floating cards/chips
- modern tech studio feel

Accent direction:

- black or near-black background
- green accent
- can include cyan/blue/purple gradient accents for a SaaS feel

The design should be inspired by modern Dribbble SaaS landing pages, especially:

- dark gradients
- rounded dark cards
- glowing accent edges
- smooth reveal animations
- polished dashboard mockups
- floating interface elements

## Tone Of Voice

Siteup should sound:

- plain
- confident
- practical
- clean
- professional

Avoid:

- startup hype
- corporate buzzwords
- overly technical AI language
- "AI revolution" style copy

Good example:

> Get a clean website, show up correctly online, and make it easier for customers to contact you.

Good AI/workflow example:

> We look at your repetitive tasks and build automations that save time.
